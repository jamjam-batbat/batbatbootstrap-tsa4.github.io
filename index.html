Blub Doodles!

Ah, the Blub.

What is a Blub?

Is it a majestic creature that somehow graced the Earth with its presence?

Or is it a being that would wreak havoc and chaos if it ever leaves its restraints, or worse...?

Nah, I'm just joking with ya! Blubs are just goofy little creatures that resemble deformed and limbless versions of humanoids, so you can get one of your own today!

Sketches!

My sketches are usually just outlines with the framework being visible underneath it. I can't exactly pinpoint how clean my linework usually is in these sketches, but I can assure you that I don't put as much effort into my inking as I do for my more serious illustrations.

Either way, you can also choose a suitable color that I can sketch your commission in!

(NOTE: There is an Additional Character Fee of PHP 100.00 if you want to add one more character. If you want to add more than that, please message the artist about it first.)

Monochrome Illustrations!

My monochrome illustrations are slightly self-explanatory: each character gets drawn and/or colored by applying black and white (and/or a suitable color) in different opacities to give out the illusion of using different colors.

Sometimes, these monochrome pieces might convey the feelings you're going for better than its fully-colored counterparts.

(NOTE: There is an Additional Character Fee of PHP 100.00 if you want to add one more character. If you want to add more than that, please message the artist about it first.)

Full Color Illustrations!

The drawing's now in full color! These illustrations only have flat colors by default, but you can pay extra for both shading and highlights to get your commission in its full glory!
